NOX

Nox is a 2,5D atmospheric puzzle platform game set in an 80's arcade. You play as Bob, a person who just went to the arcade to play some games but suddenly falls down and finds himself very tiny with only his matchbox left. Bob now has to find a way out of the arcade and it looks as if the only way to get out, is to manoeuvre his little body all the way through an arcademachine to the top.

This game was one we had to make in a group. My group existed of 4 artists and 2 programmers, of witch I was one.

Robocontest at VIVES

The goal was to follow a white line with an autonomous robot cart via one or more optical sensor(s). The competition consists of completing the progressively more difficult course as quickly and as far as possible.


The floor was black and it was a white line of 19mm thick. A bend had a minimum radius of 10 cm. There was a crossroad. In the end, the line was disrupted for a maximum of 30 cm long.

Explanation

Chid of Lothian is a groups project of about 22 students, of whitch 8 artists, 10 designers and 4 programmers. I am the AI programmer in the group.

In Child of Lothian, you play as an orphan robbed of her childhood as her family is put on trial by zealous witch hunters. You are left on the streets to escape their persecution inside the bustling world of 18th century Edinburgh.
As the AI programmer in this project I made my gole to make the city feel lively and the guards react smart to trouble makers. I will mainly focus on underlying techniques like LODs for AI, Tactival nodes and spacial partition mapping.

My Part

My part in this project was the creation of grenral NPC behavior and of the creation of Brain. Brain is the base NPC for all interactabe NPCs to come and for all witch hunters to follow. Currently Brian is located at a guate and does not want te player to pass. He will only step aside if the player completes hes quest or if he gets destracted bevause the player destroyed a lamp.

To realize this, I used enhanced the navmesh with a technique discussed in a GDC talk: Spaces in the Sandbox Tactical. Each node on the navmesh knows its neighbours and the nodes it's easy to get to, it also knows which of their nodes are visible and they have an interesting value. The interest value tells Brain if there has been something he needs to check out.
Combining that with an EQS (environment query system) I can easily steer/direct Brain where I want him to go.
Our Brain has 4 modes, the first one is "idle", the second one is "suspicious", the third one is "confirmed" and the fourth one is "find clue".
